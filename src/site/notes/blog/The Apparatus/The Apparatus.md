## The Procedure
this is our improved guide. it's not necessarily inaccurate to the original instructions, but rather, based on them and much more scientific and specific.
### WARNING: GLOVES
It is recommended to use gloves while doing [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#The Procedure\|#The Procedure]].
### Cleaning Solution
The solution is a mixture of 99% isopropyl alcohol and coarse sea salt. We're currently using x brand sea salt and 70% isopropyl alcohol, found in a common grocery store.
### Steps
1. Pour out the water in the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Water Chamber\|#Water Chamber]].
2. Remove bowl, plug one end with a finger and add a small amount of [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Cleaning Solution\|#Cleaning Solution]] and shake. if [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Bowl\|#Bowl]] is still dirty then pour solution into a cup and submerge [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Bowl\|#Bowl]]and let sit for 15 minutes.
3. Remove [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Bracket\|#Bracket]] and detach the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Glycerin Tube\|#Glycerin Tube]] from the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Water Chamber\|#Water Chamber]].
4. Add an arbitrary amount of the[[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Cleaning Solution\|#Cleaning Solution]] to the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Water Chamber\|#Water Chamber]].
5. Assemble [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus\|The Apparatus]] without the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Bowl\|#Bowl]] and attach the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Bracket\|#Bracket]]. Once secure, plug both ends with fingers and shake [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus\|The Apparatus]] aggressively until satisfied. Rinse and repeat as many times as necessary.
6. (optional step) after a heavy smoke, it is recommended to let the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Water Chamber\|#Water Chamber]] sit with solution in it for 15 minutes as well
7. rinse all [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Components\|#Components]] with water and allow to fully dry before storage. Do NOT place the [[blog/The Apparatus/The Apparatus#Glycerin Tube\|#Glycerin Tube]] in cold place to freeze until completely dry.
